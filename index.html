Hello!

I am interested in various problems in the low-resource domain, primarily machine translation. Currently, I'm also working on event classification tasks in a sparse data setting. Previously, I have worked on clickbait detection and word sense disambiguation.

I started the Master's in Computer Science program at Johns Hopkins in August 2018. Prior to that, I graduated with a B.Tech degree in Computer Science and Engineering in June of the same year. Currently, I'm the head course assistant for Object Oriented Software Engineering for Fall 2019. I previously served as a course assistant for the same course in Fall 2018 and as head course assistant for Gateway Computing: Python in Spring 2019.

Over the years, I have been involved in several notable side projects. I worked on a chat platform - Ping - that allowed users to communicate with each other regardless of language. I was involved in efforts to build a service - hello friend - that allowed people without internet access to avail various crucial facilities. Previously, I was an iOS developer for several small-scale applications.
